Hi there!

We're the University Concert Band (also known as UCB), a student-ran group that welcomes players from all levels and backgrounds. Although we do auditions for placement at the beginning of the semester, we take everyone!

UCB rehearses every Monday from 7-9PM at the Music Rehearsal Hall and holds a concert at Blair at the end of every semester. In collaboration with VCO, the Vanderbilt Commodore Orchestra, we also have a chamber program consisting of various ensemble groups and hold social events for our community. We hope you'll join us!

If you would like more information about UCB, see our audition info and fill out our interest form!
