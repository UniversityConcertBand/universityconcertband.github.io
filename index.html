Hi there!

We're the University Concert Band (also known as UCB), a student-ran group that welcomes players from all levels and backgrounds. Although we do auditions for placement at the beginning of the semester, we take everyone!

UCB rehearses every Monday from 7-9PM at the Music Rehearsal Hall and hold a concert at Blair at the end of every semester. In collaboration with VCO, the Vanderbilt Commodore Orchestra, we also have a chamber program of various ensemble groups and hold social events. We hope you'll join us!
